
# **SecureText**  
*(CryptoB)*

### **Encrypt Your Messages with Ease**

CryptoB is a user-friendly application designed to encrypt and decrypt text messages, ensuring a private conversations remain private. Whether you're sharing sensitive information or just want peace of mind, SecureText has got you covered.

## **Usage**

1. **Encrypting a Message**  
   - Open the app.  
   - Enter the text you want to encrypt.  
   - Provide a unique encryption key.  
   - Click "Encrypt."  
   - Copy or share the encrypted text file throughout any massager app or Email .  

2. **Decrypting a Message** 
   - past Key #1 (lib.txt) into app resource folder.
   - Paste the encrypted text file (cipher) into the app resource folder.  
   - Enter the matching encryption key.  
   - Click "Decrypt."  
   - View original message in file called (result.txt) in the app resource folder.

## **Security Disclaimer**

While CryptoB uses none industry-standard encryption, always ensure your encryption keys are kept confidential. Avoid sharing sensitive information over insecure channels.
